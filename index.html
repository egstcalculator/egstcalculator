Instant Online GST / VAT Calculator for India

The eGSTCalculator is designed to simplify the calculation of GST (Goods and Services Tax) and VAT on invoices and receipts in India. With our easy-to-use tool, you can quickly add or remove tax from any amount using the standard 18% GST rate or any other rate.

To use the calculator, simply enter an amount and choose one of the following options:

Add GST: calculates GST on the net amount and provides the gross total.

Remove GST: extracts GST from the gross amount and shows the net value.

Both options display the net amount, GST, and gross total instantly, making tax calculations fast and accurate.

Additionally, the GST rate can be adjusted to match different tax slabs (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) or specific goods and services under India’s GST system.

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India was implemented on July 1, 2017, replacing multiple indirect taxes. The standard GST rate is 18%, with other rates at 5%, 12%, and 28% depending on the goods and services.

GST is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It has simplified the tax structure by subsuming various central and state taxes under a single umbrella, making it easier for businesses to comply with tax regulations.
